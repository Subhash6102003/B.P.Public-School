Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school timings?

School hours are from 8:00 AM to 2:15 PM, Monday through Saturday. The administrative office remains open until 3:00 PM on weekdays and 12:00 PM on Saturdays.

When does the admission process begin for the next academic year?

Admissions for the next academic year typically begin in November, with forms available until December. Please check our Admissions page for the exact dates and detailed process.

Is school transportation available?

Yes, the school provides transportation services covering major areas around Manjhanpur. The transportation fee varies based on the distance from the school. Please contact the administrative office for route details and fees.

How can parents stay updated about their child's progress?

Parents can access the student portal using their credentials to check academic progress, attendance, and announcements. Regular parent-teacher meetings are also scheduled throughout the academic year.

What extracurricular activities are offered at the school?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (cricket, basketball, volleyball, athletics), arts (music, dance, painting), and clubs (science, literary, eco, coding). Students are encouraged to participate in at least one activity.
