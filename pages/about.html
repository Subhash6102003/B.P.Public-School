About Our School

Learn about our prestigious institution, our mission, and the values that guide us

Our Story

B. P. Public School in Manjhanpur, Kaushambi is a prestigious educational institution rated 4.3 stars based on 50 customer reviews. Since our establishment, we have built a strong reputation for excellence in the field of education.

With a comprehensive approach to education, B. P. Public School ensures that every student receives the attention and support they need to excel. Our institution is dedicated to academic excellence and operates on a schedule that accommodates students' varied commitments.

We are a leading name in the education sector, offering a wide range of programs to cater to the diverse needs of our students. With a team of experienced educators, we are committed to providing the best educational experience for every student.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures intellectual, physical, and moral development of students, preparing them to be responsible global citizens.

We strive to create an inclusive learning environment where students from diverse backgrounds can thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in a rapidly changing world.

We envision our students becoming leaders who contribute positively to society and drive positive change.

Our Values

  • Excellence in all endeavors
  • Respect for diversity and inclusion
  • Integrity and ethical conduct
  • Innovation and creative thinking
  • Social responsibility

Our Journey

2000

Establishment of B. P. Public School with just 5 classrooms and 150 students in Manjhanpur, Kaushambi.

2005

Expansion of campus facilities including new classrooms, science labs, and a computer lab.

2010

Achieved CBSE affiliation and started secondary classes. Introduction of modern teaching methodologies.

2015

Development of sports infrastructure including a multi-purpose hall and expanded playground facilities.

2020

Integration of digital learning platforms and smart classrooms across all grades.

Present

Continuing to provide quality education with over 1000 students and state-of-the-art facilities.
