Admissions

For admission to B. P. Public School, we have a structured process that ensures a fair and transparent selection of students.

Our Admission Process

B. P. Public School has a structured admission process that ensures fairness and transparency in selecting students. We welcome applications from students of diverse backgrounds and are committed to promoting inclusivity and cultural diversity.

1

Application Submission

Complete and submit the application form along with all required documents. Application forms can be obtained from the school office or downloaded from our website.

2

Document Verification

All submitted documents will be verified by the admissions team. Incomplete applications may not be considered for further processing.

3

Entrance Assessment

Applicants will undergo an assessment appropriate for their age and grade level. This helps us understand their strengths and areas for improvement.

4

Parent-Student Interview

Selected candidates and their parents will be invited for an interview. This helps us understand the child better and align educational expectations.

5

Admission Offer

Based on the assessment results, interview, and available seats, admission offers will be made to selected candidates.

6

Fee Payment

Confirm the admission by paying the required fees within the specified timeline to secure your child's seat.

Required Documents

General Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Passport size photographs (4 copies)
  • Birth certificate
  • Proof of residence (Aadhaar card/Utility bill)
  • Parent's ID proof (Aadhaar card/PAN card)

For Transfer Cases

  • Transfer Certificate from previous school
  • Character Certificate
  • Report cards of previous academic years
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)

For Special Categories

  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Disability certificate (if applicable)
  • Sports achievements certificates (if any)
  • Any other relevant certificates

Fee Structure

Our fee structure is transparent and designed to provide value for the quality education we offer. All fees are payable on a quarterly basis.

Fee Type Primary Section Secondary Section
Registration Fee (One-time) ₹2,000 ₹2,500
Admission Fee (One-time) ₹10,000 ₹12,000
Tuition Fee (Per Quarter) ₹12,000 ₹15,000
Development Fee (Annual) ₹8,000 ₹10,000
Computer Lab Fee (Per Quarter) ₹2,000 ₹2,500
Science Lab Fee (Per Quarter) NA ₹2,500
Transportation Fee (Optional, Per Quarter) Depends on distance (₹4,000 - ₹8,000)

Important Notes:

  • Fee structure is subject to revision on an annual basis.
  • A late fee of ₹500 will be charged for payments made after the due date.
  • Sibling discount of 10% on tuition fees is available for the second child.
  • Fee can be paid online through the student portal or at the school's accounts office.
