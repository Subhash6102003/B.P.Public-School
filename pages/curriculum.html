Our Curriculum

Discover our comprehensive educational programs designed to nurture future leaders

Curriculum Overview

At B. P. Public School, we follow the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) curriculum, which is recognized for its comprehensive approach to education. Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in core subjects while also encouraging critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills.

We believe in holistic development and therefore emphasize not just academic excellence but also physical education, arts, values, and life skills. Our teaching methodology is student-centered, interactive, and incorporates the latest educational technologies to make learning engaging and effective.

Our Curriculum Features:

  • CBSE-aligned comprehensive academic program
  • Focus on conceptual understanding rather than rote learning
  • Integration of technology in teaching and learning
  • Regular assessments and detailed feedback
  • Emphasis on practical learning and real-world applications
  • Values education and character development
  • Language development through various activities
  • Special programs for gifted students and those needing additional support

Academic Programs

Pre-Primary (Nursery to KG)

Our Pre-Primary program focuses on developing foundational skills through play-based learning. Children explore language, numbers, environmental awareness, and creative expression in a nurturing environment.

  • Play-based learning approach
  • Development of motor skills
  • Introduction to basic concepts
  • Socialization and emotional development

Primary (Classes I to V)

The Primary program builds on the foundation laid in pre-primary, introducing formal subjects while maintaining an engaging and interactive approach to learning.

  • English, Hindi, Mathematics, EVS/Science
  • Computer Education and General Knowledge
  • Art, Music, and Physical Education
  • Project-based learning activities

Middle (Classes VI to VIII)

The Middle School program expands the scope of learning with additional subjects and a deeper exploration of concepts, preparing students for secondary education.

  • English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, Social Science
  • Computer Science and Third Language option
  • STEM activities and practical experiments
  • Introduction to debate, public speaking, and research skills

Secondary (Classes IX & X)

The Secondary program prepares students for the CBSE Board Examination with a comprehensive curriculum and exam-oriented strategies while ensuring deep conceptual understanding.

  • English, Hindi/Sanskrit, Mathematics, Science, Social Science
  • Information Technology (optional)
  • Career guidance and counseling
  • Regular mock tests and assessments

Teaching Methodology

Student-Centered Approach

Our teaching methodology places the student at the center of the learning process. We recognize that each child has unique learning styles, strengths, and areas for growth. Our teachers employ a variety of teaching strategies to address these diverse needs.

Key Aspects of Our Methodology:

  • Interactive Learning: Engaging activities that encourage participation and discussion
  • Inquiry-Based Learning: Encouraging questions, exploration, and discovery
  • Collaborative Learning: Group activities that develop teamwork and communication skills
  • Technology Integration: Smart classrooms and digital resources to enhance learning
  • Differentiated Instruction: Adapting teaching methods to meet individual student needs
  • Experiential Learning: Field trips, experiments, and projects that connect theory to practice

Assessment System

Our assessment system is designed to be continuous and comprehensive, evaluating not just academic knowledge but also skills, attitudes, and overall development. We follow the CBSE guidelines for assessment while incorporating our own additional methods to provide a well-rounded evaluation.

Formative Assessment

  • Regular class assignments
  • Project work and activities
  • Class participation
  • Periodic tests
  • Subject enrichment activities

Summative Assessment

  • Mid-term examinations
  • Annual examinations
  • Practical examinations
  • Board examinations (for Classes X)

Co-Scholastic Assessment

  • Work education
  • Art education
  • Health and physical education
  • Discipline and values

Parents receive regular updates on their child's progress through report cards, parent-teacher meetings, and our online student information system. We emphasize constructive feedback that helps students understand their strengths and areas for improvement.

Frequently Asked Questions

What curriculum does the school follow?

B. P. Public School follows the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum, which is one of the most recognized and respected educational frameworks in India.

How are students assessed throughout the year?

We follow a continuous and comprehensive evaluation system with both formative and summative assessments. This includes periodic tests, project work, class participation, mid-term and annual examinations, along with co-scholastic assessments.

What languages are taught at the school?

English is our primary medium of instruction. Hindi is taught as the second language. For middle school (Classes VI-VIII), we offer Sanskrit as the third language option.

How does the school incorporate technology in learning?

We have smart classrooms equipped with interactive digital boards, computer labs, and educational software. Teachers integrate technology into their lessons, and students learn digital literacy, basic coding, and responsible use of technology.

What support is available for students who are struggling academically?

We offer a comprehensive Learning Support Program that includes remedial classes, individualized learning plans, additional practice materials, and one-on-one sessions with teachers. Our goal is to ensure that every student can achieve their potential.
