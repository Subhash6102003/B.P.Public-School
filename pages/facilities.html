Our Facilities

Explore the state-of-the-art infrastructure and amenities at B. P. Public School

World-Class Infrastructure

At B. P. Public School, we believe that a conducive learning environment is essential for academic excellence and overall development. Our campus is designed to provide students with modern facilities that support both academic and extracurricular activities.

From well-equipped classrooms to specialized labs, from extensive sports facilities to creative spaces, our infrastructure is carefully planned to meet the diverse needs of our students. Every facility is maintained to high standards of safety, cleanliness, and functionality.

Our Campus Features:

  • Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms with modern furniture
  • State-of-the-art science and computer labs
  • Well-stocked library with digital resources
  • Multipurpose hall for events and activities
  • Extensive sports facilities and playgrounds
  • Secure campus with CCTV surveillance
  • Medical room for emergency healthcare
  • Hygienic canteen serving nutritious food

Multipurpose Hall

Our multipurpose hall serves as a venue for school assemblies, cultural programs, seminars, workshops, and other events. It is equipped with a stage, sound system, lighting, and seating arrangements for a large audience.

  • Capacity for 500+ students
  • Modern sound and lighting system
  • Stage for performances
  • Air-conditioned environment

Music & Dance Room

Our music and dance rooms are specially designed for the performing arts with proper acoustics, mirrors, and equipment. Students learn various forms of music and dance under the guidance of trained instructors.

  • Musical instruments (keyboard, tabla, harmonium, etc.)
  • Audio equipment
  • Mirrored walls for dance practice
  • Wooden flooring

Canteen

Our school canteen provides hygienic and nutritious food options for students and staff. The menu is carefully planned to ensure balanced nutrition, and the canteen adheres to strict hygiene standards.

  • Clean and hygienic kitchen
  • Nutritious meal options
  • Comfortable seating area
  • Regular health and safety inspections

Medical Room

Our medical room is equipped to provide first aid and basic medical care for students and staff in case of illness or minor injuries. A trained nurse is available during school hours to attend to medical emergencies.

  • First aid equipment and supplies
  • Beds for rest
  • Regular health check-ups
  • Tie-up with nearby hospital for emergencies

Safety & Security

Ensuring a Safe Environment

At B. P. Public School, the safety and security of our students is our top priority. We have implemented comprehensive safety measures across the campus to ensure a secure learning environment.

  • Security Personnel: Trained security guards at all entry/exit points
  • CCTV Surveillance: Cameras installed throughout the campus
  • Fire Safety: Fire extinguishers, alarms, and regular fire drills
  • ID Cards: Mandatory ID cards for students, staff, and visitors
  • Visitor Management: Strict protocols for visitor entry and exit
  • Transport Safety: GPS-enabled school buses with trained staff
  • Emergency Protocols: Well-defined procedures for various emergencies
  • Safety Education: Regular safety awareness programs for students

Transport Facility

School Bus Service

We provide a safe and reliable transport service for students with well-maintained buses that cover various routes in and around Manjhanpur, Kaushambi. Our transport system is designed to ensure that students reach school and return home safely and on time.

  • Fleet of Buses: Modern, well-maintained school buses
  • GPS Tracking: Real-time location tracking for parents
  • Trained Staff: Experienced drivers and attendants on each bus
  • Safety Features: First aid kits, fire extinguishers, and speed governors
  • Route Planning: Optimized routes to minimize travel time
  • SMS Alerts: Notifications for parents about bus arrivals

For detailed information about bus routes, timings, and fees, please contact the school transport department or visit the school office.
